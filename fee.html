INFORMATION ON PAYMENT OF SCHOOL FEES.

Note: Parents may avail the on-line mode of payment of the school fees. Please log on to www.sxccampuscare.in.

a. The school fees for an academic year (April to March) has to be paid on a quarterly basis.
1st quarter: April, May & June
2nd quarter: July, August & September
3rd quarter: October, November & December
4th quarter: January, February & March

b. No rebate is given for school vacation.
c. Fees paid will not be refunded even if the student does not attend class or leaves the school.
d. Fees has to be paid as long as students' names are on the rolls
e. Fees not paid entail a fine of Rs. 500 for every defaulting month.
f. Fees defaulters of a quarter are not permitted to the class in the following quarter and their names are liable to be struck off the rolls.
g. Due to unavoidable reasons fees may be increased during the current academic year. If so, parents will be duly informed.

CONCESSION
Deserving students will be given fees concession in tuition fees. Concession is granted by the principal for the current academic year. Concession availed of by misrepresentation of facts is liable to be cancelled. Application form for concession is available at the principal's office. Duly filled forms must be submitted by 25 April 2017 for the current year. Fee concessions may be renewed if the student's academic progress and application is satisfactory, and discontinued if unsatisfactory or if a student repeats a class.
